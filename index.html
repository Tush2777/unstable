Our History

  1. 1998

    Founded

    Wachoro High School was established in Nairobi, with a mission to empower youth through quality education and unity.

  2. 2005

    First KCSE Graduates

    The school celebrated its first KCSE graduating class, with 92% transition to university and tertiary colleges.

  3. 2023

    Awarded Best in Nairobi

    Wachoro High School received the Best School award for academic excellence and innovation in Nairobi County.
